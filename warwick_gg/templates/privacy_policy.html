The Warwick.gg Privacy Policy

Last updated 11:48 p.m, Saturday 9th June 2018

The University of Warwick Computing Society may collect personally identifiable information about you, such as your full name, email address, and University of Warwick ID number or SU Associate Member number. In cases where you make purchases from us, we or our third party payment processors will collect your payment information. We do not share your information with other third parties.

We use this information to identify you at our events and establish that you are eligible to sign up to them. We may send you emails upon request for technical support. We may also send promotional emails, if you are an active member (have paid the membership fee to the Students Union for the current academic year), are a former member of our exec team, or have opted in to receive them.

Warwick.gg is developed as an open-source project and its source code is available to be viewed on GitHub.
