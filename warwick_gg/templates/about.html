What is Warwick.gg?

Warwick.gg is a new LAN management platform developed by the University of Warwick Computing Society for their LAN parties. It has made it possible for changes in the way UWCS organises its LANs in ways previously not considered, like allowing non-society members to sign up to LANs for a small fee.

Warwick.gg is still in active development with its source code available GitHub under an MIT license. You can also take a look at the Warwick.gg privacy policy here.

If you have any issues using Warwick.gg, please contact one of the UWCS Exec. They should be able to help you out with your problems.
